Glamping Lakeside

Experience more than a city or cultural trip in Bandung and get closer with nature by glamping in the city and surrounded area.

Set on the banks of remote Lake Patengan, this collection of simple tents is 5 km from Perkebunan Teh Rancabali (tea plantation) and 51 km from Husein Sastranegara International Airport. Straightforward tents come with mattresses, decks, lake views, and en suite bathrooms; some have boat-shaped beds. Family tents sleep up to 8 people. Room service is available 24/7. Complimentary breakfast is served in a rustic restaurant. A campsite is available.
